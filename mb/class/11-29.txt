Avery and Brevoort
Lenders covered by CRA experienced lower delinquency rates and less risky lendings
Banking institutions did not get into subprime lending

Hernandex and Murillo
Only 6% were issued by CRA regulated lendors (high-priced loans)
Higher priced lending motivated by profitability
Delinquency rates similar across incomes and neighborhoods

Fannie and Freddie
Not owned and operated by the govt, but have always been backed by the government.
Implicit agreement that if Freddie and Freddie got into trouble, the govt would make sure creditors got money.
More than 75% of subprime originated by brokers and sold to non-GSE companies.
Between 02 and 06, F&F lose market share (54 -> 40%)
